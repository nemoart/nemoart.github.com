3. Photorealistic effect 

As above gallery samples have shown, NEMOART’ rendering makes your 3D creation into reality. Using several materials such as glass, plastic, and mirror, your 3D creation seems to be genuine with proper exposure. This is one of the most powerful weapons in NEMOART. NEMOART generates natural light as if your 3D creation is originally in the photo. Therefore, you just put your own 3D creation into photo in order to make a natural image. Your 3D creation is not a simple 2D emoticon anymore. It is reusable and recreative in various dimensions and in different spaces. These works are attributed to the following effects. In other words, it means that your 3D creation can be set in any position, and it can be adjusted to any sizes according to your photo composition. If it is created on your hand, it can be free anywhere. 

•  Realistic material 
•  Natural light
•  Reflection effect
•  Refraction effect
•  Shadow effect

Material
NEMOART supports several materials, which means that you can assign different materials to different cubes. Even though you make the same figure model, the rendering images depend on the materials which you choose. Your 3D model can be air, glass, metal and so on. It can also be light as well.


Natural light
In real life, the same object looks different depends on where it is. If the object is under the hot sun, it seems to be vivid. On the other hand, the object is under the candlelight, it seems to be dim. NEMOART also provides the natural light based on the light source of your photo. Therefore, the same model can be appeared differently depending on the photo that you use.

Reflection
If you make a floor based on the reflective materials such as metal or mirror, you can see that another 3D creation of yours can be visualized on the floor with defined reflection. Furthermore, the objects that were originally in your photo also show reflection on the floor as well. This reflection effect makes 3D creation more realistic. 


Refraction
If you are using glass or air material, you can create refracted effects of your 3D creation so that it can show the back side of it. 


Shadow
If you use shadow effect in NEMOART, one can finally achieve your 3D creation to perfection. You can make an actual shadow of your 3D creation in your photo. Most people won’t be able to differentiate whether it is real or not.
